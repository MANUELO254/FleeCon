The Founder

You'll deal with me, not a call centre.

I've been on building sites since before most contractors started using WhatsApp to dodge client calls. Over a decade of foundations, frames, finishes and everything in between — residential, commercial, renovations, roofing. Done it all, and I'm still doing it myself.

What I've learned is that most building problems aren't technical. They're about someone not saying what needed to be said early enough. The wrong tile ordered because nobody confirmed. The budget running away because "that wasn't in the quote." I've seen it happen to people I like, and I decided early on that wasn't going to be how I work.

So when you call me, I come. When I look at your site, I tell you what I see — the good, the expensive, and the things you hadn't thought of. And when we agree on a price and a timeline, that's what it is.

No drama. No surprises. Just a builder who shows up and does what he said he'd do.

David Kiarie Founder & Director, Fleecon Kenya Ltd

How We Work

What you can expect from us.

Not a mission statement. Just the things we actually do, every single project.

Straight Talk

We tell you what your project actually needs — and what it'll actually cost. If the budget doesn't match the brief, we say so before we start, not halfway through.

Quality That Lasts

We don't build for the handover — we build for ten years from now. The materials, the methods, the finishes. Done once, done properly.

Respect for Your Time

We show up when we say we will. You get updates before you have to ask. And if something is going to take longer, you hear it from us first.

One Relationship

You deal with the same person from first call to handover. Not a salesperson, then a project manager, then someone you've never met finishing your floor.

We Come to You

Karen, Kiambu, Naivasha, the coast — if you have a plot and a plan, we'll come look at it. Our work is all over Kenya and we're comfortable working anywhere in it.

Properly Registered

NCA-registered, insured and compliant. When you hire Fleecon, you're hiring a contractor the industry recognises — not someone building with crossed fingers.

The Journey

How we got here.

2012

Started on the Tools

Began working on residential sites in Nairobi — learning the trade from the ground up. Concrete, blocks, frames. Every stage, hands-on.

2015

First Independent Projects

Took on first solo contracts — small renovations and finishing jobs in Karen and Westlands. Built a reputation for showing up and doing it right.

2018

Fleecon Kenya Ltd Registered

Formalised the business, obtained NCA registration, and started taking on full new builds alongside renovation work. The name comes from the work: filling the gaps others leave.

2020

Expanded Across Kenya

Started working outside Nairobi — Kiambu, Naivasha, the Rift Valley. Project management work for clients who needed one trusted person coordinating multiple trades.

2023

100+ Projects Completed

Passed the 100-project milestone — residential, commercial, renovation and roofing work across 47 counties. Most clients came through referrals.

Now

Still Building

180+ projects done. Still personally involved in every one. Still answering the phone. Still telling clients what they need to hear, not just what they want to.
