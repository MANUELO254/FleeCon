Old space, new life.

Tired floors, dated kitchens, walls in the wrong places — we strip back, rebuild and finish so it feels like a brand new home without the brand new price tag.

We work with what's there.

Most renovations go sideways because the contractor finds something unexpected behind a wall and the budget runs away. We don't work like that. Before we lift a hammer, we do a proper walk-through and tell you what we can see — and what we might find when we open things up. So when something does come up, we already had a conversation about it.

We renovate around your life. Need the kitchen working while we do the bathrooms? Said. Want one room finished before you move back in? Sorted. We sequence the work so you're never living in chaos longer than you have to.

From a single-room refresh to a whole-house gut job, the standard is the same: clean site, honest updates, work that lasts.

What's included

  • Pre-strip site assessment
  • Demolition & safe disposal
  • Plumbing & electrical re-routing
  • Wall & floor reconfiguration
  • Full finishing & clean handover
