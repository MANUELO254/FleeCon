Not sure where to start? Sit down with us.

Honest advice, fair estimates, no obligation. Whether you're scoping a dream build, second-guessing a quote you've been given, or just trying to figure out if your idea is realistic — we'll talk it through.

The cheapest thing we offer — and the most useful.

A lot of building decisions get made in a panic. Someone hands you a quote, you don't know if it's reasonable, you say yes because saying no feels harder. Then halfway through the job, you realise you should have asked more questions before signing.

That's where we come in. Even if you've already hired someone else — bring us your plans, bring us another contractor's quote, bring us photos of the site. We'll tell you straight what's reasonable, what's missing, and what to push back on. No catch, no sales pitch.

If you end up hiring us, great. If you don't, you walk away knowing more than you did. Either way is fine with us.
