The details that turn a house into a home.

Plaster that lays flat, tiles that line up, paint that doesn't peel after one rainy season. The finishing is where we don't cut corners — because that's exactly where everyone else does.

Where quality shows up.

You can build the strongest house in Kenya, but if the plaster cracks in six months and the grout lines wander, people don't see the structure — they see the cracks. Finishing is the part that everyone walks past and judges first, and it's the part most builders rush.

We don't rush it. We mix our own plaster on site so it cures right for the climate. We lay tiles by eye and by laser. We prime properly before we paint, and we use paint rated for Kenyan sun and rain, not just whatever's cheap that week.

Whether you're calling us in for the whole finish package or just to fix what someone else messed up, we'll leave it looking like it should — and lasting like it should too.

What's included

  • Plastering & skim coats
  • Floor & wall tiling
  • Painting (interior & exterior)
  • Trim, skirting & cornices
  • Cabinetry & built-ins
  • Snag-list fixes & touch-ups
