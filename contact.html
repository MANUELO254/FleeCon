Common Questions

Things people usually ask.

How quickly can you come look at my site?

Usually within the same week for Nairobi and surroundings. For upcountry sites we'll agree a date that works — we don't make you wait long for a first visit.

Do you charge for quotes?

No. The site visit and estimate are free. You only pay when we agree on a scope and sign off on it together.

What areas do you cover?

We're based in Nairobi but work across Kenya. We've done projects in Kiambu, Naivasha, Nakuru, Mombasa and plenty in between. If you have a site, we'll come to it.

Can you work with my existing architect or engineer?

Absolutely — and we prefer it when clients come with plans. We work well alongside other professionals and won't try to replace anyone who's doing a good job.

What's the best way to send photos of my site?

WhatsApp is the easiest — send photos, a short voice note, whatever makes sense. You'll get a response the same day in most cases.

Do you handle the council approvals and permits?

We can guide you through what's needed and refer you to the right people. For full permit processing, we work with trusted practitioners who handle that efficiently.
